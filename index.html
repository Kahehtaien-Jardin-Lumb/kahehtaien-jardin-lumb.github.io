Kahéhtaien Jardin Lumb is a Chinatown-based collective garden in Tiohtià:ke (Montreal),
born out of a collaboration with Projets Autochtones du Québec.

about kjl

about kjl

As a collective garden, we aim to nurture an intergenerational and intercultural space rooted in decolonial values.Through the practice of growing Asian and Indigenous herbs and vegetables, we provide a space dedicated to community-led activities and empower marginalized communities such as seniors, Asian, and Indigenous peoples through hands-on knowledge exchange.

Our name reflects the spirit of connection and respect that defines the garden.
→ Kahéhtaien: “at the place of” in Kanien’kéha.
→ Jardin: means “garden” in French.
→ Lumb: honors Janet Lumb, a long-time artist, community organizer, and advocate for Montreal’s Chinatown’s preservation and its green spaces.

save kjl: petition to stay in chinatown

These last months have been feeling quite unstable at the Kahéhtaien Jardin Lumb, your local Asian-Indigenous collective garden situated in Montreal's Chinatown. For the past three years, the lot has been leased to the garden by the City of Montreal under a temporary lease set to end in December 2026. At the same time, the site has been put up for sale and is being considered for off-market housing development. In other words, this garden, which has become a beloved gathering space for the Chinatown community, may be displaced as soon as an off-market housing project is approved. We need your support to help protect this space.

season 2026: call for proposals

We'll be opening our doors on may 23rd! This year, the Kahéhtaien Lumb Garden Collective wishes to enrich its community programming by highlighting community-led initiatives! We are offering individuals or groups the opportunity to propose an activity for our upcoming season. We are accepting both one-time news (ex. a collective cooking or traditional medecine workshop, a movement or popular education activity, etc.) and recurring news (ex. a tai chi or knitting night every Tuesday).
